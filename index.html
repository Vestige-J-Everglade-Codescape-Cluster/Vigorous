Summarized Explanation of Vigorous:

'Vigorous' takes place in an enigmatic world starting from within the country of 'Libertalon' , following a boy named Draco who suffers a horrible fate due to the corrupt government. He's lost his parents, his attachments, and his old life. The only things he has left are his good friend Quincy, and his burning rage that eventually fades.

Almost a decade later when he's 18, his hate for the government is reignited after a series of events which spiraled from an encounter with some tyrannical police. Both he and Quincy come across the people who call themselves 'vigilantes' , specifically the squad of 'The Vestiges'.

Draco and his friend are then offered the choice to join them; finally, a chance for revenge. Accepting, his decision would completely alter the path of his life, sending him on an adventure in which he learns many things about the world and fights against creatures and people, rotten to the core or dead inside. But no matter where he goes, where he thinks, where he acts, or even breathes, he realizes that the most powerful thing he's grown is the ability-

to be vigorous.
